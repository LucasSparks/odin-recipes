How to make Scrambled Eggs

Ingredients

  • Eggs (however many you want)
  • Salt and pepper
  • Cooking oil or butter

Instructions

  1. Lightly beat the eggs in a medium sized bowl
  2. Salt and pepper eggs
  3. Add cooking oil or butter to pan on low heat
  4. Swirl pan to coat bottom and sides with the oil or butter
  5. Cook eggs slowly while scraping them up with a spatula on occasion
  6. Cook til most of the liquid has thickened
  7. Serve while hot
