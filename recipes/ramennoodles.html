How to make Ramen Noodles

ingredients

  • Packet of instant ramen noodles
  • Soy sauce
  • 1 onion

Instructions

  1. Place water in pot while on high heat.
  2. Dice onion.
  3. Once water begins to boil add ramen noodle block.
  4. Place diced onion in pot.
  5. Lower to medium heat.
  6. Ocasional stir ramen noodles to break block up.
  7. Remove from heat once the noodles are the texture you like.
  8. Drain water.
  9. Add flavor packet with soy sauce and enjoy.
