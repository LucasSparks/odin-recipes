How to make Poor mans meal

Ingredients

  • 6 Potatoes
  • 4 hotdogs
  • 1 onion
  • Cooking oil you like
  • About 2 tablespoon of salsa you like
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Dice potatoes, chop onion and place in frying pan
  2. Pour small amount of cooking oil on top of onion and potatoes
  3. Turn heat down to medium
  4. Mix around every few minutes til potatoes brown
  5. Once potatoes are tender and onions are cooked add the cut hotdogs and salsa
  6. Gently mix while adding salt and pepper to taste
